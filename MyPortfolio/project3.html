More About My Project

I have been passionate about inspiring my younger generations to STEM field and teaching them about the process. To do so, the very first thing I could try on my own was teaching them the basics of STEM which is Robotics.

This program will allow youths to understand and apply basic real-world skills while learning and developing habits in a team environment. It is offered to anyone with an access to a computer and that wants to learn the foundations of coding and robotics!

By doing this program, I have inspired more than 15 students to learn more about STEM field and I'm still working towards inspring more youths.
