More About My Project

We Mongolia is a nonprofit organization who stand strong for woman in tech.

I find joy in helping others. Under my leadership, my team produce and broadcast podcasts to share college admission resources and other essential information for high schoolers. In addition, I actively engages in raising public awareness of gender inequality and the challenges that girls and women face today. Recently, in celebration of the International Day of the Girl, I spearheaded the pre- production stage of the “Digital Divide” awareness video content as a lead of team GenZ.
